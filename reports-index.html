Why This Cleanup Matters
Before Cleanup
"Online Booking" was treated as a lead source, mixing marketing attribution with tool usage. Impossible to know if Google Ads or Facebook drove those bookings.
After Cleanup
Lead Source = "Google Ads", Capture Method = "Online Booking". Now you can see that Google Ads + Online Booking converts at 82%.
Pro Impact
Pros can finally answer "Is my marketing working?" and optimize spend based on real ROI data, not polluted attribution.
Leadership Value
Proves ROI of booking products (Online Booking, CSR AI) with clean conversion data and revenue attribution.
